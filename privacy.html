PRIVACY POLICY

Your trust is our top priority. Here’s how we protect and handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the essential information needed to provide our services effectively and safely. This includes:

  • Personal Data: Name and email address you provide when submitting forms (contact or certification).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you navigate and interact with our site, including page visits and AI Chat interactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data responsibly for purposes such as:

  • Responding to inquiries and support requests.
  • Processing certification registrations.
  • Analyzing site usage to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Maintaining and improving AI Chat and other services.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to safeguard your data. While no online system is 100% secure, we process information responsibly and avoid storing sensitive data wherever possible.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party providers for hosting and form processing. When you submit forms, your data is handled securely by the service providers. We encourage reviewing their privacy policies for complete transparency.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our site may use minimal cookies for analytics and performance monitoring. These do not store personal information and are used only to enhance your experience.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us anytime via the Contact page to exercise these rights.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact page. We take your privacy seriously and respond promptly.

By using Daminī Codesphere, you agree to this Privacy Policy and the responsible handling of your information.
