PRIVACY POLICY

Your trust is our priority. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide and improve our services and community. This includes:

  • **Personal Data:** Name and Email address when you submit a contact or certification form.
  • **Usage Data:** Information on how you access and use the site (e.g., page views, time spent, interaction with the AI Chat).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries (via Formspree).
  • To process certifications.
  • To monitor and analyze site usage to improve content and features.
  • To maintain the functionality of our services (e.g., the AI Chat).

3. Data Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We do not store sensitive information; data is securely processed via Formspree.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Vercel (hosting) and Formspree (form submission). Your data submission through our forms is governed by the privacy policies of Formspree.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Contact page.
